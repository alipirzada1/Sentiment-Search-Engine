Introduction

Sentiment is defined as a person's opinion regarding anything. It can be either Positive Neutral or Negative. Twitter is one of the most popular Social Media Website where people tweet about a lot of stuff and give their opinion regarding different things.

Sentiment Search Engine is a Search Engine that takes tweets from Twitter and classify them as Positive Neutral or Negative.

Let’s suppose a company launches a new product and after some time they want to check the success/failure of their product. What they can do is they can Search the name of their product in our search engine and it will pick up live tweets (150 in number) from twitter and classify them as Positive Neutral or Negative and will also provide a summary of how many people are in favor or against their product. The user will be able to see read the comments as well.


Methodologies

Lexicon Based Approach

Also called Dictionary Based Approach in this approach we have a dictionary containing polarity values for different words. After Preprocessing of each Tweet from Twitter we take polarity values from that dictionary and then sum them up to calculate the overall sentiment of the sentence.

Machine Learning Approach

In this Approach we provide a model to the machine and then train the machine to classify tweet. The model we have used is called Bag of Words Model and the Machine Learning Algorithm we have used is called Support Vector Machine (SVM)
